    • Reinventing Meaning - Lion & Macaw

    This project intended on using the initially positive characters that represented freedom, independence, hope, and perserverence as symbols to draw attention to severe issues in the world. In this case, deforestation in Kenya and Brazil became the issues that would be highlighted by the popular chaarcters Simba (The Lion King) and Blu (Rio).

    The concept for this came from the fact that scenes in these movies often took place in forested areas, such as Simba's time in the jungle and Blu's experience in the Amazon rainforest. Many of these situations could not have happened if they were affected by our modern problems. This design took illustrations and used their intentions as characters to depict the sadness, harm, and severity of deforestation, a real world problem.
